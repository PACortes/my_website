Context

Evaluating university faculty teaching and research performance is necessary for multiple reasons, including assurance of student learning and informing administrative decision-making.

In this workshop, we will use a simulated dataset consisting of faculty employees information. The data set will be use for learning data visualization in R. The dataset is available in ´xlsx´ and you can download it here.

Content

The dataset contains 20 variables and 311 rows of data (number of faculty employees). It reports data metrics related to faculty´s demographics, salary, teaching and research performance, among others.
