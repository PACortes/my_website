Context

Evaluating university faculty teaching and research performance is necessary for multiple reasons, including assurance of student learning and informing administrative decision-making.

In this workshop, we will use a simulated dataset consisting of faculty employees information. The data set will be use for learning data visualization in R. The dataset is available in ´xlsx´ and you can download it here.

Content

The dataset contains 20 variables and 311 rows of data (number of faculty employees). It reports data metrics related to faculty´s demographics, salary, teaching and research performance, among others.

Data Dictionary

Variable Datatype Description
EmployeeName Character Faculty’s full name
Salary Numeric The faculty´s yearly salary. $ U.S. Dollars
Sex Character Sex - M or F
MaritalDesc Character The marital status of the faculty (divorced, single, widowed, separated, etc)
DaysLateLast30 Numeric The number of times that the faculty was late to work during the last 30 days
Absences Numeric No. of times the faculty was absent from work during the year
Age Numeric Age
YearBirth Numeric Faculty’s year of birth
Nationality Character Faculty’s nationality
School Character School of faculty´s appointment
Course Numeric No. of courses instructed during the year
Appointment Numeric Appointment ranks - 1 = Assistant Professor, 2 = Associate Professor, 3 = Professor
Satisfaction Numeric Results from the last labour satisfaction survey - Value between 0 to 5
ResearchBudget Character Annual Research Budget
FirstYear Numeric Year of firts appointment
Articles Numeric No.of articles per year published in peer-reviewed journals during the year
Students Numeric No.of supervised theses during the year
Evaluation Numeric Results from the last Teacher evaluation survey - Value between 0 to 5
